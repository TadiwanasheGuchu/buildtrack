# BuildTrack

## Overview

BuildTrack is a modern construction project management platform designed for small and medium-sized construction companies across Southern Africa. The platform helps contractors, site managers, workers, and clients collaborate efficiently by managing projects, budgets, materials, tasks, and progress updates in one centralized system.

The goal is to replace fragmented workflows that rely on WhatsApp groups, spreadsheets, paper records, and phone calls with a professional digital solution that improves transparency, accountability, and project delivery.

## Problem Statement

Many construction businesses struggle with:

- Poor project visibility
- Budget overruns
- Material shortages
- Inefficient communication
- Lost paperwork
- Limited client transparency
- Difficulty tracking worker productivity

BuildTrack addresses these challenges through real-time project management and reporting tools.

## Target Users

### Construction Company Owners

Monitor projects, budgets, expenses, profitability, and overall business performance.

### Site Managers

Manage daily operations, workers, materials, tasks, and progress reporting.

### Workers

View assigned tasks, update progress, and record attendance.

### Clients

Track project progress, review reports, view photos, and approve milestones.
